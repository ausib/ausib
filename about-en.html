Company Profile

AUS International Business, the parent company of AUSIB Cross-Border Shopping, was established in Sydney in 2015, with warehouses located in the main storage area of the inner-west region of Sydney. Since 2016, we have been providing seamless direct shipping and fulfillment services, offering convenience to cross-border e-commerce platforms and agents. Our company primarily focuses on renowned Australian and New Zealand products, directly sourcing from manufacturers and brand suppliers. Our main sales model revolves around dropshipping and wholesale. In addition to modern supply chain methods, we also maintain traditional supply modes, including general trade and OEM processing services. We supply products to local retailers in Australia and export to overseas markets, including mainland China, Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, and bonded zones.

Business Direction

We are a cross-border e-commerce company based in Australia, primarily engaged in overseas purchasing, import trade, and cross-border e-commerce operations, with a focus on providing direct shipping services to China. Our main product categories include, but are not limited to, baby and maternity products, health supplements, cosmetics, skincare products, clothing, and electronics.
